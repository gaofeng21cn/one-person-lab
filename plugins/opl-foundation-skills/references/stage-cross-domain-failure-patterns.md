State: `support_reference`
Machine boundary: This reference is shared prose for OPL foundation stage Skills. It is not a schema, validator, owner receipt, typed blocker, quality verdict, candidate body rule, readiness gate, or runtime authority.

Use these MAS/MAG/RCA/BookForge-derived patterns as review heuristics inside stage Skills. Each Skill maps the pattern to its own finding classes and output shape.

- `critique_as_repair_hint`: A critique may suggest a repair hint, missing proof, review question, owner workorder, or route-back wording. It does not execute repair, mutate a projection, pass/fail a gate, certify replay, close a monitor, accept/reject a candidate, sign an owner receipt, create a typed blocker, or decide promotion/readiness.
- `source_or_receipt_stale`: Stale source, monitor, provenance, runtime event, owner receipt, visual export, grant package, publication evidence, or book memory/artifact refs cannot support current claims until the owning surface refreshes them and binds them to the current identity.
- `owner_route_overclaim`: Human-review routes, domain owner refs, owner-evidence refs, connector receipts, OPL evidence refs, route-back packets, replay-success refs, or owner-routed workorders are not owner acceptance, quality verdict, typed blocker, human decision, publication/grant/visual readiness, domain readiness, or production readiness.
- `candidate_body_reconstruction_forbidden`: Do not reconstruct or judge hidden candidate, artifact, source, clinical data, grant text, visual artifact, manuscript, prompt, or domain bodies from refs, counts, hashes, metrics, rankings, summaries, negative paths, lifecycle labels, or evidence lower bounds.
